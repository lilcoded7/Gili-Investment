Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Prestige Wealth?
Getting started is simple. Create an account on our platform, complete the verification process, select your preferred investment plan, and make your initial deposit. Your dedicated wealth manager will then contact you to discuss your investment strategy.
What security measures are in place?
We employ bank-level security including AES-256 encryption, multi-signature wallets, cold storage for 95% of assets, regular security audits, and 2FA for all accounts. Your investments are also insured against theft and hacking incidents.
How are returns generated?
Our returns are generated through a combination of algorithmic trading, staking, yield farming, and strategic long-term holdings. Our team of experts continuously monitors market conditions to optimize performance across all strategies.
How often are profits paid out?
We distribute profits according to your selected investment plan. Silver Tier investors receive quarterly payouts, Gold Tier monthly, and Platinum Tier investors receive weekly distributions. All payouts are automatically sent to your designated wallet.
Can I withdraw my funds at any time?
Yes, you can request withdrawals at any time. Standard processing takes 1-3 business days. Certain investment strategies may have specific lock-up periods which will be clearly communicated before you invest.
