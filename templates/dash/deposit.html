How to Deposit

To deposit into your Prestige Wealth account, follow these steps:

  • Click the "Make Deposit" button below.
  • Select your preferred cryptocurrency.
  • Enter the amount you wish to deposit.
  • Copy the wallet address from the Deposit History table.
  • Send the exact cryptocurrency amount to the wallet address.

Processing Time: Deposits are typically credited within 24–48 hours after 3 network confirmations. If your deposit is not credited within this timeframe, please contact our support team.
